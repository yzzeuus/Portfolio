About Me

The Designer
Behind the Systems

I'm a Gameplay Designer with a track record shipping live content to over 100 million players on Fortnite Battle Royale. I own features end-to-end — from initial concepting and prototyping in Unreal Engine to live tuning, economy design, and post-launch iteration.

My work spans weapon design, in-game economy systems, IP collaborations (Marvel, Power Rangers), and progression mechanics. I love working at the intersection of player psychology, mechanical depth, and live-service realities.

Currently based in London, UK and open to new opportunities.
